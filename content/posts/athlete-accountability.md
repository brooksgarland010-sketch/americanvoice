Athletics is built on measurable outcomes, peer support, and direct feedback. Those habits translate well to civic work: prepare thoroughly, show up consistently, and report results with clarity.

## Three transferable habits

1. Practice with intention and clear metrics.
2. Create feedback loops that are candid and respectful.
3. Review performance without defensiveness.

These habits are not partisan. They are practical, and they build trust.
